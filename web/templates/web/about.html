About

MINIPRINT is application made for community to summarize long legal paragraphs in their own words. While providing summaries they can rate each legal paragraph is it good or bad for customers/users as well as rate other peopleâ€™s summaries and start discussions about them.

Companies can upload their legal documents on MINIPRINT where they can manage them and integrate them on their website. When their customers register to their site legal documents will be prompted in engaging and easy to digest manner.

Process

1 Company uploads their legal document on MINIPRINT

2 Document get parsed into paragraphs.

3 MINIPRINT users summarize each paragraph in short and meaningful way

4 Trough voting process, we can detect harmful parts of legal documents and select best summaries for each part
